Creating a new entity
1 Once a category is created (Refer to page "Creating a new category" for more details), its scaled image will be shown in the right side of the application editor. Drag the corresponding icon.

Tip: In the case of two categories having same image, to ensure the desired icon is dragged, you can move the mouse over icons, the category name will appear in the right side of Messages area.



2 Drop the icon into the simulation world, showing all existing system entities.
